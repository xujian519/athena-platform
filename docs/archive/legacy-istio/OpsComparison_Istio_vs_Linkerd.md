Operations Comparison - Istio vs Linkerd

- Installation & Upgrades: Linkerd often simpler; Istio requires more configuration.
- Troubleshooting: Istio has broader tooling; Linkerd's tooling is lighter.
- Ecosystem/Community: Istio has larger ecosystem; Linkerd has dedicated but smaller footprint.
- Observability integration: Both provide telemetry, but approaches differ.
