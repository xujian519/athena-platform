## 🔥 重点论文推荐

### 1. SAGE: Scalable Agentic 3D Scene Generation for Embodied AI
- **arXiv ID**: [2602.10116](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10116)
- **作者**: Hongchi Xia, Xuan Li, Zhaoshuo Li, Qianli Ma, Jiashu Xu, Ming-Yu Liu, Yin Cui, Tsung-Yi Lin, Wei-Chiu Ma, Shenlong Wang, Shuran Song, Fangyin Wei
- **提交日期**: 2026-02-10
- **领域**: 计算机视觉 (cs.CV), 机器人 (cs.RO)
- **核心内容**: 提出SAGE，一个智能体框架，用于根据用户指定的具体任务自动生成仿真就绪的3D环境
- **项目页面**: https://nvlabs.github.io/sage
- **PDF**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.10116.pdf

### 2. Trustworthy Agentic AI Requires Deterministic Architectural Boundaries
- **arXiv ID**: [2602.09947](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09947)
- **作者**: Manish Bhattarai, Minh Vu
- **提交日期**: 2026-02-10
- **领域**: 密码学 (cs.CR)
- **核心内容**: 提出Trinity Defense Architecture，通过三种机制强制执行安全性：操作治理、信息流控制、权限分离
- **PDF**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.09947.pdf

### 3. Grounding Generative Planners in Verifiable Logic: A Hybrid Architecture for Trustworthy Embodied AI
- **arXiv ID**: [2602.08373](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.08373)
- **作者**: Feiyu Wu, Xu Zheng, Yue Qu, Zhuocheng Wang, Zicheng Feng, Hui Li
- **提交日期**: 2026-02-09
- **领域**: 人工智能 (cs.AI), 机器学习 (cs.LG)
- **核心内容**: 提出VIRF (Verifiable Iterative Refinement Framework)，一个神经符号架构，实现确定性逻辑导师与LLM规划器的协作
- **会议**: ICLR 2026
- **项目页面**: https://openreview.net/forum?id=wb05ver1k8

### 4. AgentCgroup: Understanding and Controlling OS Resources of AI Agents
- **arXiv ID**: [2602.09345](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09345)
- **作者**: Yusheng Zheng, Jiakun Fan, Quanzhi Fu, Yiwei Yang, Wei Zhang, Andi Quinn

- **核心内容**: 系统分析AI编码代理的OS级资源动态，提出eBPF资源控制器AgentCgroup

### 5. Puda: Private User Dataset Agent for User-Sovereign and Privacy-Preserving Personalized AI

- **领域**: 人工智能 (cs.AI)
- **核心内容**: 提出Puda，一个用户主权的架构，支持三级隐私控制

### 6. Fork, Explore, Commit: OS Primitives for Agentic Exploration
